ABOUT ME

"Why should I receive a scholarship?"

I was excited to read about the Udacity-Google scholarship program that will provide world-class learning opportunity for Africans. My goal is to be a “Professional Developer” and to build websites and applications that will meet market standard whilst being useful in making Nigeria and Africa better.

I am a degree holder in Biochemistry with innate talent in Computing. I could not study Computer Science for some unforeseen circumstances. Nonetheless, I started out by teaching myself through the Internet; How to Code, Troubleshoot Softwares, work with offline/online servers and how to use softwares like Wordpress, Adobe Suite, Microsoft Office, and Text Editors in creating functional designs.

Few years from now, I see myself solving crucial technological issues and building user friendly apps. This program will give me the exact skill set I need to succeed at this. I need this opportunity to move my career forward, to learn from the best, to be a true force in this field and to fulfil my dream of becoming a Professional Developer. The scholarship program will solely push me further towards having a successful career and helping Africa embrace the internet positively.

It will be a delight that I am given this opportunity to be awarded a scholarship from the best online computer school to help fulfil my dream. The field is challenging and one that has difficult courses; however, I feel that I will have no problem arising to this challenge. I appreciate your consideration.



So far, I have interned at Greenmouse Technology, I have also subscribed to online tutorials e.g. W3School, Udacity, Youtube tutorials etc. I was also involved in organising a State Computer Math Quiz Competition at Delta State Innovation Hub.

I am currently learning with Udacity in preparation for this programme whilst acquiring some basic skills that can help me have better understanding of this field.

In preparation for this program, I have completed the under listed courses from Udacity within the past 3 weeks:

  • Intro to HTML and CSS.
  • Android Basics: User Interface.
  • Intro to Javascript.

Also, my knowledge of the three courses above helped in designing this Website.
